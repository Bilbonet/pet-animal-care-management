Main Features

  * Client Information
  * Pet Information
  * Boarding Agreement
  * Day Care Reservation
  * Booking For Pet Service

Client Information

Here, You can see the client form. And you can see own Pet's details, boarding History, day care history and service history.

Pet Information

Here, You can see the pet information form.

Boarding Agreement

Here, You can see the boarding agreement form. And also you can send the request for a pet boarding.

Day Care Reservation

Here, You can see the day care reservation form. And also you can send the request for a pet daycare.

Booking For Pet Service

Here, You can see the pet service form. And also you can send the request for a pets services.

Report

Here, You can see customer invoice report. And you can also generate boarding pet category wise and day care pet category wise report.

Access Control

There are two access control for User and Manager.
